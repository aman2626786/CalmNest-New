Box Breathing
Beginner
A simple technique to calm your nervous system and reduce stress.
5-10 minutes

How it Works

Evens out breath pace, stimulating the vagus nerve to lower heart rate and blood pressure.

Steps

  • Inhale for 4s
  • Hold for 4s
  • Exhale for 4s
  • Hold for 4s
4-7-8 Breathing
Intermediate
Known as the "relaxing breath," it helps with anxiety and sleep.
3-5 minutes

How it Works

Acts as a natural tranquilizer for the nervous system by increasing oxygen in the bloodstream.

Steps

  • Inhale for 4s

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
Beginner
Pulls you out of anxious thoughts by focusing on your senses.
5-7 minutes

How it Works

Redirects focus from internal distress to the external environment, interrupting overwhelming thoughts.

Steps

  • See 5 things
  • Feel 4 things
  • Hear 3 things
  • Smell 2 things
  • Taste 1 thing
